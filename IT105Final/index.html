History

Back in 1930 in the boonies of Australia, Angus Angerman and his son Lucas Alexander Panek were surviving in the outback when they discovered a ruby red spider that created the softest and silkiest thread ever known to man. This father son duo with their small loan of $20,000,000,000,001 they were able to purchase 4 acres of land which they began to breed this new spider. They experienced some initial success but that success was turned around by the Black Friday Bushfires which devestated the entire operation. Angus Angerman sadly passed away during this wildfire and Lucas was left to bring this company back from ruin. After receiving another loan for $50 by the King of New Zealand, Lucas was able to purchase 20,000 acres to grow his spider empire. Lucas then sold his company to Amazon which they rebranded to the Spiders R Us you all know and love.
